About Me

Heyyaa! I am John Matthew Reyes, an aspiring game developer and a massive a game enthusiast with experience in immersive and interactive games. Being a game enthusiast isn't really just about playing any kind of games games; it's a deep deep deep appreciation for the art, strategy, and storytelling that the game has to offer. Whether you're immersed in the combat and open worlds of RPGs, or competing in a fast-paced action of shooting games, or strategizing in the depths of something like board games, being a game enthusiast means embracing the diverse and evolving landscape of gaming. Beyond the thrill of gameplay,  I of such often find myself engaged in discussions about game mechanics, industry trends, and the latest releases of the gaming technology.
